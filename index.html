Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about OmniSubs

Do I need an account to use OmniSubs?

Yes, you need an account on OpenSubtitles.com to use OmniSubs. The registration is free and only takes a minute.

Is there a limit to how many subtitles I can download?

Free users of OpenSubtitles.com have a daily download limit, which may result in errors if you attempt too many downloads in a single day. Premium accounts have higher or unlimited download limits.

Which video formats are supported?

OmniSubs works with most common video formats including MP4, MKV, AVI, WMV, and more. If your video file has proper metadata, OmniSubs should be able to find matching subtitles.

How secure is my OpenSubtitles.com account information?

Your login credentials are stored locally on your machine and are only used to authenticate with OpenSubtitles.com. OmniSubs does not send your information to any third parties.

Which languages can I translate subtitles to?

With Gemini translation integration, you can translate subtitles to virtually any major language. The translation quality depends on the language pair and the clarity of the original subtitles.
